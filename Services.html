Our Services

At KSOFT, our multi-skilled teams deliver advanced process and design solutions using industry-leading platforms such as Siemens, Rockwell, Delta, and Mitsubishi. We provide premium-quality PLC and SCADA implementation services to clients across the globe.

Our industrial automation offerings include seamless integration of factory lines with intelligent systems like recipe management, reporting and productivity monitoring, databases, Factory Information System, Production Management System, and Manufacturing Execution Systems.

Headquartered in Sangli, Maharashtra (India), KSOFT delivers factory automation, system integration, and programming services to customers worldwide. We offer a comprehensive range of automation engineering solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of various industries.
